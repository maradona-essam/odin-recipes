Spicey Macaroni

Spicey Macaroni Plate

Ah, spicy macaroni with tomato sauce. Is there anything quite as delicious when you're craving pasta with a kick to it?

We certainly can't think of anything! That's why we've put together this easy, delicious recipe for you. Follow the steps below to make delicious, spicy macaroni with tomato sauce you won't be able to get enough of.

Ingridents

  • 3 cups dry macaroni elbows
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/4 to 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • 6 ounces water
  • 135 grams canned tomatoes or tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon lemon
  • 4 coriander leaves
  • 1 red onion
  • 2-3 cloves garlic
  • 1/4 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper for seasoning
  • 1 cup beef or chicken stock, cubed
  • Slices of toast (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil water and add about 1/2 cup of macaroni [as required].
  2. While the macaroni boils, pour a little oil to the frying pan and add diced onion and garlic and sauté well till they turn slightly brown.
  3. Add can of tomatoes/tomato paste and add red chili powder, cumin powder and coriander powder and sauté well till the sauce turns thick.
  4. Now add the macaroni and sprinkle salt and pepper and mix well.
  5. Sprinkle a little lemon juice and coriander leaves and serve with bread or toast.
